features showcase

click each option below to get familiar with the key features of the app.

the timer

just sixty seconds seperate each elimination. try to make your judgements quickly and be sure to vote before time expires. you never know, it could be you on the chopping block.. see the faq

the vote

no electoral college here, circle is pure democracy. the user with the most votes at the end of the round is kicked. if there's a tie, someone is selected randomly! see the faq

the shields

feeling the heat? activate a shield before time expires and guarantee your safety into another round. each user gets one shield everyday or if you're particularly unpopular, shields can be purchased in packs of 3. see the faq

the slate

your slate is the record of your game history. depending on when you get kicked, your star ranking will go either up or down. superficial or not, remember, that your star ranking affects who you can add as a friend/message. see the faq
